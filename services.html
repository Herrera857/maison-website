i.

ii.

Reservations

Restaurants, hotels, and private experiences across the EU. The Michelin tables, the bistro the locals actually go to, the boutique hotel with the right view. We use our names so you don't have to.

Custom days with the guides, drivers, sommeliers, and curators we trust — most of whom we've sat at a table with. No bus, no group, no script. Wine country, museums, gardens, kitchens.

How It Works

A simple, old-fashioned process.

First, a conversation. Email, phone, or video — in English or French. We learn what you love, what you've already done, what you'd like to avoid. There's no charge for this and no obligation.

Then, a proposal. Within a week, you receive a draft itinerary with our recommendations — hotels, routes, experiences, restaurants, pace. We iterate until it's right.

Then, the work. We handle the bookings, confirmations, dietary notes, special occasions, transfers, tickets, and the dozens of small details that turn a good trip into a great one.

Then, the trip. You travel with our phone number in your pocket. We are reachable, always. If something goes sideways — a flight, a closure, a change of mood — we sort it.
